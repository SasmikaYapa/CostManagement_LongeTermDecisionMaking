Capital Budgeting Calculator

Capital Budgeting is the process businesses use to evaluate large investments. Techniques like Net Present Value (NPV), Internal Rate of Return (IRR), and Payback Period help determine if an investment should be made.

The Net Present Value (NPV) calculates the present value of future cash flows to determine profitability. The Internal Rate of Return (IRR) calculates the discount rate that makes NPV zero. The Payback Period determines how quickly the investment recovers its initial cost.
